Nintendo Game Boy

The Nintendo Game Boy is a retro handheld gaming console that defined portable gaming. Released in 1989, it is loved by millions of gamers worldwide for its nostalgic gameplay and design.

  • Durable and portable design
  • Classic games like Pokémon, Tetris, and Super Mario Land
  • Battery life of up to 15 hours
  • Game Link Cable support for multiplayer fun

Specifications:

  • Screen: 2.6-inch monochrome LCD
  • Resolution: 160 x 144 pixels
  • Weight: 220g
  • Dimensions: 90 x 148 x 32 mm
