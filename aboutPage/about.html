Who are we?

Here at Nintendo, our main goal is to put smiles on faces all around the world. We started at 1889 making simple playing cards, now we are making video games and now we have a successful company.

Nintendo as a name was established in 1980 in Redmond, Wash. And we are committed to delivering the best content possible.

Our history

1889

Fusajiro Yamauchi started manufacturing "Hanafuda" playing cards in Kyoto, Japan.

1980

The Game & Watch product line released

1981

A coin-operated arcade machine of the video game donkey kong was launched and distributed

1985

The Nintendo Entertainment System Launched.

1989

The Game Boy launched and is the first portable handheld game system and launched with Tetris
